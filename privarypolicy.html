Secure Vault - Privacy Policy

Note: This is a demo website, no real data is stored. DO NOT ENTER YOUR PERSONAL DETAILS!

Effective Date: 2 November 2023

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Passwords you store: Only the passwords you enter in the app.
  • Website names or usernames: Corresponding to your stored passwords.
  • Usage data (optional): Such as pages visited or actions within the app.

Note: We do not collect your passwords for any external use. All data is stored locally in your browser unless you implement a server.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Store and display your saved passwords securely within the app.
  • Allow features like copy, view, and delete password.
  • Improve the functionality of the website.

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses localStorage to save your passwords in your browser. No cookies are used for tracking purposes. All data is stored locally and not transmitted externally.

4. Data Security

Your passwords are stored locally in your browser. We recommend you use strong master passwords and keep your device secure. We are not responsible for data loss if your device is compromised.

5. Third-Party Services

Currently, we do not integrate any third-party services that collect personal data. If you add analytics or other services later, we will update this privacy policy.

6. Your Choices

You can delete all stored passwords at any time by clearing your browser localStorage. You can also disable features like saving passwords in your browser if desired.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with the effective date updated.
